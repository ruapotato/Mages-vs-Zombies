NIGHT MECHANICS (CRITICAL!):
-----------------------------
Zombies become TERRIFYING at night - players MUST seek shelter!

Night Multipliers:
- Damage: 2.0x (double damage to player!)
- Speed: 1.5x (50% faster movement)
- Health: 1.5x (50% more HP)
- Spawn Count: 2.0x (waves spawn 2x zombies)
- Spawn Rate: 1.5x (spawn 50% faster)

Visual Changes:
- Day: Greenish tint (0.6, 0.8, 0.6)
- Night: Reddish tint (0.8, 0.5, 0.5) - menacing!

Console Warnings:
- "NIGHT HAS FALLEN! Zombies are now 2X stronger, 1.5X faster, with 50% more health!"
- "Waves will spawn 2X MORE zombies! SEEK SHELTER!"

PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATIONS:
--------------------------
- Batch processing: Only 20 path updates per frame
- LOD system: 
  * Full detail < 30 units
  * Medium detail 30-60 units  
  * Minimal detail > 60 units
- Physics limit: Max 100 zombies with full physics
- Animation limit: Max 400 zombies animating
- Frustum culling: Invisible zombies teleport (no physics)
- Can handle 400+ zombies at 60 FPS!

- Max active zombies: 50
- Signals: wave_started, wave_completed, all_zombies_defeated

================================================================================
QUICK START GUIDE:
================================================================================

1. SETUP:
   - Ensure NavigationRegion3D exists in your map
   - Ensure player is in "player" or "local_player" group
   - DayNightCycle autoload must exist (already in project.godot)

2. ADD ZOMBIE HORDE TO SCENE:
   var zombie_horde = ZombieHorde.new()
   add_child(zombie_horde)

3. SPAWN ZOMBIES:
   zombie_horde.spawn_zombies(10)  # 10 random zombies
   zombie_horde.spawn_zombies(5, "brute")  # 5 brutes

4. ENABLE WAVES:
   zombie_horde.enable_waves = true
   zombie_horde.wave_interval = 120.0

5. CONNECT SIGNALS:
   zombie_horde.wave_started.connect(_on_wave_started)
   zombie_horde.zombie_died.connect(_on_zombie_died)

6. TEST NIGHT MODE:
   DayNightCycle.set_time(0.0)  # Midnight
   # Watch zombies become MUCH stronger!
