# Complete Spell List

## Fire Spells (4)

### 1. Fireball (Tier 1)
- **Type:** Projectile
- **Damage:** 50
- **Mana Cost:** 15
- **Cast Time:** 0.3s
- **Cooldown:** 0.5s
- **Effects:** Explodes on impact (2m radius), applies burn (10 DPS for 3s)
- **Description:** Launch a flaming projectile that explodes on impact

### 2. Flame Wave (Tier 2)
- **Type:** AOE
- **Damage:** 35
- **Mana Cost:** 25
- **Cast Time:** 0.5s
- **Cooldown:** 3.0s
- **Effects:** Expanding wave (8m radius), applies burn (15 DPS for 4s), knockback
- **Description:** Unleash a wave of fire that spreads outward

### 3. Inferno (Tier 3)
- **Type:** AOE
- **Damage:** 80 + 20 per tick (0.5s interval)
- **Mana Cost:** 45
- **Cast Time:** 1.5s
- **Cooldown:** 8.0s
- **Duration:** 6s
- **Effects:** Persistent fire area (10m radius), applies burn (30 DPS for 6s)
- **Description:** Create a raging inferno that burns all within

### 4. Meteor Storm (Tier 4)
- **Type:** AOE
- **Damage:** 120 per meteor
- **Mana Cost:** 60
- **Cast Time:** 2.0s
- **Cooldown:** 10.0s
- **Duration:** 5s (12 meteors, one every 0.4s)
- **Effects:** Rain meteors in 15m radius, 3m explosion radius, applies burn (25 DPS for 5s)
- **Description:** Rain fiery meteors from the sky

### By Type
- **Projectile:** 9 spells
- **Beam:** 2 spells
- **AOE:** 9 spells
